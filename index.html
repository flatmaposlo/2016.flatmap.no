CALL FOR SPEAKERS

We invite you to contribute to this community by giving a talk or a workshop at flatMap(Oslo).

The scope of submissions is broad at flatMap(Oslo). We're happy to hear about for example field reports, new libraries, new techniques, type astronautics, alternative languages, or something completely different — Whatever you're passionate about.

That said, we are nudging the balance a bit towards more broadly accessible subjects this year by accepting more talks on applied functional programming.

Talks are 40 minutes long. Workshops are negotiable, but normally one or two slots. Note that this year we enforce a max one gif rule for all talks.

We accept proposals continuously until April 3rd. Don't wait until last minute, we might be fully booked by then.



Send your proposal to flatmap@arktekk.no

LOCATION

flatMap(Oslo) 2016 will be held at Teknologihuset - House of Communities.

Teknologihuset is situated in Pilestredet 56, not far from the city center in Oslo.

The goal for this year is the same. Small and intimate, but with high ambitions.
